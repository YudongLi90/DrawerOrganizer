Measure your drawer

Measure the inside of the empty drawer — not the outside. The organizer sits inside, so wall-to-wall inner dimensions matter.

L = inside length · W = inside width · H = inside depth (top of drawer to bottom).

Not sure? Round down a millimeter or two — the organizer needs to slide in.
