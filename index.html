Measure your drawer

Measure the inside of the empty drawer — not the outside. The organizer sits inside, so wall-to-wall inner dimensions matter.

  • Measure at 3 spots per dimension, report the smallest reading — front, middle, and back for L; left, middle, and right for W; all four corners plus center for H. Take the smallest reading from those 3 repeated measurements and round down to the closest mm value.
  • Always round down to the smallest reading (in mm). To ensure easy installation.
  • Watch for screws or hardware inside the drawer. Any protruding screw head must sit at least 10 mm above the drawer bottom
